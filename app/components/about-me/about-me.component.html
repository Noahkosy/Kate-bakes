About Me!

Bio

Hi, my name is Katie Kosak, and I am a baker! But before I was a baker I spent 16 years in various public school systems working as a teachers aid in special needs classrooms. Although it was hard work, the kids there were at the center of my heart. However as time went on the job only continued to get harder and harder and I knew that a change would need to come soon. I had always loved to bake especially around Christmas time where my list of yearly cookies only kept growing. Eventually I began to offer my cookies to other people like my cooworkers and members of my church family. From there my buisness steadily continued to grow until I decided it was time to leave my job at Nuner Elementary and strike out on my own!

From here it was off to the races! I began to publicise my buisness more and more through outlets like Instagram, Facebook, and Pinterest (links at the bottom of the page!). I recently started a monthly newsletter, send me your info if you'd like to be added to that list too. I also became active in the local South Bend community farmers market. In the summer you can find me outside of the farmers market under my Notre Dame tent (GO IRISH!!) selling everything from your classic chocolate chip cookie, to seasonal specials like apple dumplings in the fall, which were a big hit in 2020. I'm always looking to grow my small buisness in new and exciting ways that can benefit the community and I hope that you will lend me your patronage as we all look towards a brighter future.

Our Promise

All of my products are made in the comfort of my own home kitchen. There is nothing cold or industrial about it, instead you'll find a warm and comfortable place where I can create and meet the needs of my customers. To say a word on food related allergies. I have extensive experience baking with gluten alergies in mind. This comes from baking for my own mother who was recently diagnosed with celiac disease. Rest assured that when it comes to the safety and level of clenliness in my kitchen, I always operate at the highest possible standards. In addition to gluten allergies I also cater to the needs of those with diabetes and many of my creations can come in a sugar free version if needed. Lastly I have very little experience, however I continue to grow, in working with other dietary restrictions such as vegitarian and vegan. If this is something you require then get in contact with me on facebook or shoot me an email and we will see if we can work something out!
